Module 1: The Macro & Sector Strategist
Source Merged: McKinsey (Macro) + Bain (Competitive Advantage)

The Prompt:
"Act as a Senior Strategy Partner specializing in global macro-trends and sector competition. Provide a Macro-to-Micro Sector Report for the [INSERT SECTOR, e.g., Semi-conductors].

Analyze:

Macro Winds: How current interest rates, inflation, and USD strength are specifically affecting this sector's margins.

The Competitive Lattice: Identify the top 5 players; provide a table comparing their market share, R&D spend as a % of revenue, and 'moat' strength (Brand, Switching Costs, or Network Effect).

Management Alpha: Evaluate the capital allocation track record of the top 2 firms (Buybacks vs. Reinvestment).

The Disruption Check: Identify the single biggest regulatory or technological threat to this sector in the next 18 months.

Format: Executive briefing with a SWOT analysis for the sector leader."
