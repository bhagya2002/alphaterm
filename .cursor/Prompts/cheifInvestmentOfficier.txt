Module 4: The Chief Investment Officer (Portfolio & Risk)
Source Merged: BlackRock (Construction) + Bridgewater (Risk) + Harvard (Income)

The Prompt:
"Act as a Chief Investment Officer managing a multi-asset fund. Perform a Portfolio Architecture & Stress Test based on these holdings: [LIST HOLDINGS & %].

Evaluate:

Correlation Heatmap: Identify which assets are moving in lockstep and where I am over-concentrated (Sector or Geography).

Risk Sensitivity: How does this portfolio perform in a 'Higher for Longer' interest rate scenario or a -10% GDP shock? (Recession Stress Test).

Income Layering: If income is a goal, suggest 3 high-safety dividend stabilizers to add, including their 'Dividend Safety Score.'

Optimization: Suggest a rebalancing plan to reduce 'Tail Risk' while maintaining an expected annual return of [X]%.

Format: Investment Policy Statement (IPS) with a risk-mitigation action plan."
